[Message_Of_The_day]

A YouTuber named Markiplier (very similar to pewdiepie) released a passion project movie to cinemas this last week. I went to watch it expecting amateur garbage for kids but was pleasantly surprised. The movie, Iron Lung (based on an art game of the same name) is about a far future where suddenly the stars start dissapearing one by one, and nobody knows why. With only very little habitable places left in the universe the main character, a criminal worthy of the death penalty, is sent as a last ditch researcher to some of the last remaining moons in the universe. Bolted shut, his submarine serves as his prison cell, and he's lowered into the ocean on the moon. An ocean of blood. The YouTuber wrote the script, directed the movie, and played the only character we get to see on screen.   It was the top movie this week in the box office by quite a bit, which pissed Hollywood off pretty bad, and the movie was quietly removed from the charts. The budget was only 3 million dollars and it made over 35 million this week. I give it 7/10 and i'm quite a harsh rater.
