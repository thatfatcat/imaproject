[Message_of_the_day]

My struggle as of late has been feeling validated without other people. I've never done things that make me happy with myself. Everything I've done in life wasn't for me enjoying what I do, but for me enjoying others for what I do. This has been my motivation for every single achievement. This might be an unpopular opinion, but is that really so bad?

This is true for me on a romantic perspective. I've never dated anyone for myself, I've only dated people because I liked how happy I made them. My mindset for every relationship I've been in is "It doesn't matter how this makes me feel, this person deserves to be happy because they are wonderful and I love them." If I could dedicate my life to someone, even suffering for them, just so they will without knowing of my pain have a happy life, I would be fulfilled. Is that really so bad?
